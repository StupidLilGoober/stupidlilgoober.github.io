## About Me

I am a programmer who enjoys making things in C++ and rarely
doing some web projects. If you read the title at all, you'd
know that my name is *Dominic Kolp*.

Local to a household of nerds, I grew up fascinated with
video games and Kaiju films. Amongst my favorite games were
the 'classic' Sonic the Hedgehog games. By 5th grade, I had
planned on creating a Sonic Fan Game. Of course, I knew the
first thing I'd have to do was buy a PC, so I started saving,
but in the meantime I made Scratch games on random accounts
I have lost (but it's okay, since I never made an account on
one of those).

Since then, I have grown fond of arena shooters, which are
unfortunately past this time, but have given me loads of
entertainment.

I have planned to make a video game with my friends, but it's
more of a promise to do it than us actually making it. But,
since I am making a game engine, it's not that unrealistic.

## Definitions

"Cookies" are any form of data saved on your device by the website.
This includes, but is not limited to, the actual technical definition
of cookies, as defined by [Webster](https://www.merriam-webster.com/dictionary/cookie).

"User" refers to **you**, the person viewing this website.

## Your Rights

The site gives you the option to disable all unnecessary cookies, but
by using this website you agree to using "required cookies" (cookies
that are needed for the site to function properly, or to save your
choice on cookies).

This website does not sell personal information to third parties.

Users may disable non-essential cookies through the site's cookie
preferences, where available.

## Information Collected

This website may collect limited technical information automatically,
including but not limited to:

* Browser type
* Operating system
* Referring pages
* Approximate geographic region
* Pages visited and time spent on the site

This information is used only for site functionality, analytics,
security, and improving the website experience.

It also saves data that you choose to save, such as settings data.
